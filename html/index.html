       [PROJECT] Riemann Sums Visualization (js)
       [DESC]    A tool for educators and students to visualize Riemann sums;
                 the foundation of integral calculus. Use the slider to change

                 the bottom right or by pressing space.
       [URL]     https://riemann-sums.github.io
